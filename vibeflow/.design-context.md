# VibeFlow Design Context

## Service Overview
- **Name:** VibeFlow
- **Domain:** Relationship-based Wellness & Shared Hormone Tracking

## Target Audience
- Couples, friends, and families looking to share their wellness journey in a supportive, non-intrusive way.

## Brand Personality
- Warm, emotional, supportive, clean, and unobtrusive.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Combination of Clean & Calm + Warm & Cozy.**
- Emphasis on whitespace, rounded corners, warm/ivory background tones, and soft accent colors for the 4 hormones (Dopamine, Serotonin, Endorphin, Oxytocin).

## Typography Strategy
- Korean & English support.
- Needs to feel both modern (Clean) and approachable/emotional (Cozy).

## Color Strategy
- **Backgrounds:** Off-white, Ivory, or Warm Gray.
- **Accents:** Softened, pastel-like or warm variants of the 4 hormone tokens.
